Ethereum Computation Market

What is it?

The computation market is a service for Ethereum that allows for verifiable off-chain execution of computations that would be otherwise prohibitively expensive to execute within the EVM.

Documentation Source Gitter
Status: Early Alpha

The computation marketplace should be considered alpha level software. Developers interested in implementing algorithms for the marketplace should join the gitter channel
