Ethereum Computation Market

What is it?

The computation market is a service for Ethereum that allows for verifiable off-chain execution of computations that would be otherwise prohibitively expensive to execute within the EVM.

Documentation Source
Development Status

The computation marketplace is currently under heavy development and should be considered alpha level software.
